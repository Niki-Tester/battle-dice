How To Play

The aim of the game is to defeat your opponent, by rolling the highest on the die.

If you roll the highest, you will deal damage to your opponent causing them to lose health.

If your opponent rolls higher than you, you will take damage and lose health.

The damage dealt is equal to the difference between the two rolls. So if you roll a 6 and your opponent rolls a 1, you will deal 5 damage to your opponent.

Each of you will continue to roll your dice, until either of you have 0 health and are defeated.

Endless Mode

Once you have defeated all four dungeon bosses, you will have the option to continue in Endless Mode, or return to the Main Menu.

If you return to the main menu, and press Play, you will be returned to the game, in Endless Mode.

If you would like to Start Over with a new character, you can reset your game data, in the Settings menu.

Character Creation

Pressing Play in the Main Menu, will take you to the character selection screen.

You will need to enter your name or username, this must be 3 or more characters long, but no loner than 12 characters.

If you have played previously your character selection, and name will be saved, and you can continue playing from where you left off.

If would like to reset your profile, use the "Clear Data" button found in the "Settings" menu
